Our Story

About 10 years of experience

We have an outstanding story behind

Constart Company is a General Contractor that was founded in 2010. We are one of the most successful construction companies on the market. We have accomplished more than 50 big and small projects, and this number continues increasing.

Our dedicated team of estimators, designers, project managers, and builders puts hard work in each of the projects. We always make sure the building is as properly and correctly designed as possible.

Being a customer-oriented company, our customers’ needs are always in the center of everything we do. We want our customers to enjoy the process just as much as the final outcome.
